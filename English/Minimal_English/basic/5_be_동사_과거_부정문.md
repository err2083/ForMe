# be 동사 과거 부정문

나는 ~이 아니었다

과거에 대한 부정문을 만들기 위해서는 be동사 과거형 was, were뒤에 not을 붙임

|||
|---|---|
I was a teacher | I was not a teacher
She was happy | She was not happy
The were in the officee | The were not in the office

|주어+be동사|not|나머지|
|---|---|---|
|I was|not|a teacher|
|She was|not|a teacher|
|They were|not|in the office|
