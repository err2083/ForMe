# feel + 형용사

~라고 느끼다 표현

## Expression

feel은 느끼다라는 뜻으로, feel + 형용사 표현은 개인의 감정이나 느낌을 나타냄

so나 very 같은 부사를 형용사 앞에 넣어서 느낌을 한층 강조할 수 있음

* I feel sad
* I feel good
* I feel excited
* I feel fantastic
* He feels nervous
* She feels very anxious (그녀는 매우 걱정하고 있어요)
* I feel sorry
* I feel so great
* I feel safe here
* I feel very angry

## 스크립트

Today is my birthday.

I think my family and friends are going to give me a lot of presents.

I feel excited about the gifts.

But then, no one told me "Happy birthday" today.

I think no one known that it's my birthday today.

How can this be?

I feel so lonely.

I arrived home feeling sad.

There was no one at home.

But when I opened the door to my room, my friends shouted out, "Happy birthday!"

I feel happy now
