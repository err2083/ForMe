# I will

"~할 것이다" 표현

## Expression

의지를 담은 미래의 일, "앞으로는 뭄엇을 할 것이다" 공언

"will"의 부정형은 "will not"으로 "~하지 않을 것이다"

* I will treat you next time
* I won't make the same mistake
* I will always love you
* I won't miss the game
* I will be more careful
* I won't sell it to anybody
* I will wait for you
* I won't tell anybody
* I will be quit
* I won't forget about it
