# I must

"~해야만 한다" 표현

## Expression

"I must"는 의무를 나타내는 표현으로, "I have to" 보다 더 강력한 표현으로 꼭 하지 않으면 안되는 일에 대해서 must를 사용

"I must not"은 "나는 ~하면 안 된다"라는 뜻으로 해서는 안 되는 일을 나타내는 표현

* I must be on time
* I must go see a doctor
* I must keep an eye on my little brother
* I must not make a mistake
* I must not loes the game
* I must not forget to do my homework
* I must work hard
* I must pay the money by tomorrow
* I must not be late
* I must not leave this room

## 스크립트

Last night, I was on my way back home

Suddenly, I felt like going to the bathroom

I thought, "I must go to the bathroom"

I was walking to the nearest public toilet

But then, I saw another man running!

I wanted to go faster than him.

So, I thought to my self, "I must run faster!"

That guy started running faster, too!

So I ran faster, and... Bam!

We bumped into each other

He looked at me and said, "Oh goodness. I forgot to turn the oven off. I must return home quickly!"

---
on one's way 가는 중인

bump into ~에 부딪히다

turn off 끄다
