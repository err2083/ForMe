# I need to

"~할 필요가 있다" 표현

## Expression

어떤 목적을 달성하기 위해 그 일을 해야할 필요가 있다는 의미

"I have to", "I must"처럼 의무의 표현으로도 사용할수 있지만, 의무보다는 필요를 강조하는 표현

한글로 해석할때는 "~해야겠다"로 하는게 더 자연스러움

* I need to find a map
* I don't need to work on weekends
* I need to practice writing
* I don't need to worry
* I need to see my professor
* I don't need to spend money on taht
* I need to change my clothes
* I don't need to take the class
* I need to check again
* I don't need to go to the bank

## 스크립트

A new semester begins

New textbooks, new classes...

I want to do well this semester.

So, I need to study tonight.

But it's very hard to focus on studying

My desk is too messy.

The stuff on my desk is annoying!

So, I need to clean my desk.

I put away the pencils and books.

I make my desk shiny.

Ah, now this is good for studying

But then, I suddenly feel tired.

I need to get some sleep.
