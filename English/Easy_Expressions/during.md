# during

"~동안에" 표현

## Expression

"during"은 ~동안에라는 뜻으로 특정한 기간 동안 어느 상황이나 행동이 계속 진행된다는 것을 의미

"during"은 기간을 나타내는 명사와 함께 사용. "weekend", "night", "vacation" 같이 특정한 기간이나 사건을 나타내는 단어를 사용

* It rained during the night
* I went on a trip to Europe during my vacation
* I stayed in Canada during the winter
* We ate and drank a lot during the game
* Don't go outside during the storm
* Don't talk during the exam
* I watched TV for three hours during the weekend
* What were you doing during the meeting?
* I studied hard during the last semester
* I did not look at the time during the test

## 스크립트

I went to the movies with my boyfriend.

I thought the actor was funny.

I told my boyfriend I liked him.

He said, "Don't talk during the movie!"

After the movie, we went to dinner.

I sent my brother a text message

My boyfriend got angry again.

He said, "Don't text during dinner!"

The next week, my boyfriend got mad at me.

He was upset that I didn't call or text him

He asked, "What did you do during the weekend?"

I told him, "I went to the movies with my brother, and we ate a lot for dinner."
