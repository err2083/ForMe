# I have to

"~해야 한다" 라는 표현

## Expression

"I have to"는 의무를 나타내는 표현으로, 꼭 해 해야 하는 일이 있을 때 사용. to 뒤에 동사원형을 붙여 표현

"I don't have to"는 "~하지 않아도 된다"라는 뜻으로 "~하지 말아야 한다"가 아니니 주의해서 사용

* I have to clean my room
* I don't have to clean my room
* I have to solve these math problems
* I don't have to solve these math problems
* I have to return this book to the library
* I don't have to return this book to the library
* I have to wait for an hour
* I have to ask my teacher
* I don't have to go back to school
* I don't have to take care of this

## 스크립트

There is a swimming contest next week.

All of my classmates will join the contest.

So, I have to join too.

But actually, I don't really enjoy swimming.

I am a slow learner, so I still don't know how to swim well.

But to be honest, there is another serious reason I don't want to go.

For the swimming contest, I have to wear a swimsuit.

But I don't want to wear a swimsuit, because i am fat.

I'm worried! I think people will make fun of me.

I should do something.

I have to lose weight.
