# enough

"충분히" 표현

## Expression

enough는 "충분히"라는 뜻으로 동사나 형용사 뒤에 위치

enough를 활용한 대표적인 표현중에는 "A enough to B"로 "B를 할 만큼 충분히 A하다" 의미

* I didn't sleep enough last night
* He is strong enough to move the sofa
* I am old enough for this movie
* I was lucky enough to get the ticket
* You haven't tried enough
* I am hungry enough to eat everything
* I ate enough
* It is close enough to walk
* these boxes are not big enough
* I am not clever enough to solve the problem

## 스크립트

My sister and I went shopping in a mall and found a beautiful dress.

It was very fancy.

"I like that dress. Let me buy it for you!" (my sister said.)

But I said, "I have enough dresses." I just didn't want to buy it.

But I agree that it was a wonderful dress, though.

She read my face and said, "Isn't it pretty enough to wear to a Christmas party?"

I replied, "Umm... yeah... but..." I had to tell her the truth.

"Actually, it's not big enough for me."
