# It's + 날씨

## Expression

영어로 날씨를 표현하는 방법은 weather 라는 단어를 사용할 필요 없이, It's 뒤에 날씨를 나타내는 단어만 붙여주면 됨

warm, hot, cold, rainy, snowy, windy 등 날씨에 관한 다양한 형용사를 알아두기

* It's sunny
* It's warm
* It's rainy
* It's very snowy outside
* It's boiling hot
* It's cloudy
* It's cold
* It's humid
* It's foggy
* It's chilly

## 스크립트

A long time ago, there were the Wind, the Sun, and the Storm.

They wanted to decide who was the strongest.

Their challenge was, "Who can make a traveller take off his coat?"

The Wind blew fast

The traveller said, "It's windy"

Then he tightened his belt

So after, the Storm tried.

The Storm tried to take off the traveller's coat.

"It's stormy"

And he put on another coat just to keep warm

Lastly, it was the Sun's turn.

The Sun's heat was enough to make the traveller warm.

Then the traveller took off his coat

"It's too hot."
